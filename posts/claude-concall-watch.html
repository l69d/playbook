Concall Watch

A weekly earnings-call transcript tracker for top US, Indian, and Chinese companies — summarized locally on Ollama, saved to Obsidian.

iWhat it is

Earnings calls are the single best source of information about a company. But sitting through transcripts is time-consuming, and most summaries cost money or require sending transcripts to a third-party API.

Concall Watch flips this: it's a Claude Code skill that fetches the latest earnings-call transcripts for your watchlist, runs them through a local Ollama model (no data leaves your machine), and writes structured summaries to your Obsidian vault. You get a searchable, dated record of what management is saying, quarter by quarter.

1Quick start

Claude Code
# Install, then run weekly:
/concall-watch

# It will:
# 1. Fetch transcripts for top US/IN/CN companies
# 2. Summarize each via Ollama (qwen2.5:3b or your choice)
# 3. Write structured notes to Obsidian

Requires Ollama running locally with a model of your choice. Clone from github.com/l69d/claude-concall-watch.

2Why local AI

Earnings transcripts contain material non-public information in the sense that you're reading them before they're fully absorbed by the market. Sending them to a cloud API means your watchlist and its timing are visible to a third party. Running Ollama locally keeps everything on your machine — the model, the summarization, the notes. No data leaves your network.
