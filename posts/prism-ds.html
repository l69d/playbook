prism-ds

A holistic, interactive place to learn data science from EDA to production ML — with visual explanations that actually make concepts click.

iWhat it is

prism-ds is an interactive data science learning site. 12 modules covering 90 concepts — and every single one is live and built. Not a placeholder in sight.

The core idea: DS concepts are inherently visual (distributions, correlations, decision boundaries, loss landscapes), yet most courses explain them with static diagrams or walls of equations. Prism builds interactive SVG visualizations for each concept — you tweak a slider, the distribution shifts; you move a slider, the decision boundary redraws.

2Standout features

12 modules, EDA → production

Statistics, probability, visualization, feature engineering, model evaluation, ML algorithms, deep learning, deployment — a complete pipeline.

Basic / Advanced toggle

Each concept has two levels. "Basic" gets you job-ready. "Advanced" takes you to interview depth — without overwhelming beginners.

A/B test simulator

Interactive simulator that shows statistical significance, power, and sample-size tradeoffs in real time — the hardest concept in DS, made visible.

/sandbox graphing playground

A "data scientist's Desmos" — plot distributions, transform data, and explore mathematical relationships with live feedback.
