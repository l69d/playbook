ds-roadmap

An interactive, research-backed job-readiness sprint — pick your timeline and get a day-by-day plan with hand-curated resources from MIT, Stanford, and industry.

iWhat it is

ds-roadmap solves the most common question in data science: "I have a background in X, how do I break into DS — and how long will it take?"

Three plan tracks, each with per-day localStorage-tracked progress:

15-Day Sprint

Crash course for someone with strong adjacent skills (SWE, stats) who needs to be interview-ready fast. Every hour counted.

30-Day Build Month

A structured month of project-based learning. Build a portfolio while covering fundamentals.

60-Day Full Path

"Fundamentals to Hired." For career-changers with less adjacent experience. Goes deep on foundations before accelerating.

2What's inside

132 courses

25 from MIT OCW, 30 from Stanford, 77 industry sources — all hand-curated, not a firehose of links.

158 resources

Books, papers, videos, and interactive tools — categorized by topic and difficulty.

20-agent build

The 30- and 60-day plans were built by a 20-agent Workflow that researched MIT OCW, Stanford, and industry syllabi.

Progress tracking

Per-plan localStorage checklists. Know exactly where you left off and what's next.
