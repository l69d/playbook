healthbridge

A multilingual health literacy assistant powered by Gemma 4 — bridging language gaps in healthcare information across Indian languages.

iWhat it is

healthbridge was Karthik's submission to the Kaggle Gemma 4 Good Hackathon. It addresses a critical problem in India: healthcare information is overwhelmingly available in English, while a large portion of the population is most comfortable reading in their regional language.

The assistant takes health queries or documents in English and provides clear, accurate translations and explanations in multiple Indian languages — not literal translation, but culturally appropriate health literacy. It's built on Gemma 4, Google's open-weight model family.

2What makes it different

Culturally appropriate

Not just word-for-word translation. healthbridge adapts health guidance to local contexts — dietary advice, common remedies, regional health priorities.

Multilingual

Multiple Indian languages supported, with the Gemma 4 multilingual backbone providing native-level fluency rather than English-overlay.

Open weights

Built on Gemma 4 — fully open model, deployable anywhere. No data leaves your infrastructure.
