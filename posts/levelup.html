levelup

Paste a paragraph about your career, get tiered job listings you can land today — plus the next tier up with the exact skills to bridge the gap.

iWhat it does

levelup rethinks job search. Instead of searching by keyword, you paste a paragraph about your experience and goals. It uses an LLM of your choice (Claude, GPT, DeepSeek — bring your own key) to find and tier jobs:

LAND

Roles you're ready for right now. Your skills match the requirements — these are the applications to send today.

STRETCH

Roles where you meet ~60% of the requirements. A month of focused prep and you're competitive.

LEAP

Roles 1-2 levels above. The precise skills you need to add are listed — no vague "grow your career" advice.

Built with Next.js 16 and the Vercel AI SDK. Multi-provider BYOK — bring your own API key for Claude, GPT, or DeepSeek.
