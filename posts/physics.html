physics

Interactive visualizations designed to make physics concepts intuitive — the kind of diagrams and animations that turn "I think I get it" into "oh, that's obvious."

iWhat it is

Physics education has a visualization problem. Textbooks use static 2D diagrams for dynamic 3D concepts. Equations describe relationships, but building intuition requires seeing those relationships in motion.

physics is a collection of interactive visualizations that bridge that gap. Each one focuses on a single concept — a field, a wave, a particle trajectory — and lets you manipulate parameters to see how the physics changes in real time.
