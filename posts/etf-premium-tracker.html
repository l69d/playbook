ETF Premium Tracker

Live dashboard of ~326 NSE ETFs: premium/discount to NAV, time-series price & volume action, and AI-powered sentiment — all refreshed daily.

iWhat it is

Indian ETF investors face a persistent problem: ETFs trade at a premium or discount to their underlying NAV, and that gap can erase your returns. Most brokers don't show this clearly.

ETF Premium Tracker fills the gap. A Next.js dashboard that shows every NSE-listed ETF with:

NAV premium/discount

Is the ETF trading above or below its net asset value? The core metric, front and center, color-coded.

7D / 1M / 2M / 3M views

Price and volume action across four timeframes — spot trends, momentum shifts, and unusual volume.

Ollama sentiment

Summarized market sentiment for each ETF from recent news, generated locally via Ollama — no API key, no privacy leak.

2How it works

A Python pipeline runs daily (via launchd at 08:10 IST) fetching NSE and yfinance data, computing NAV premiums, and generating sentiment summaries. The result is committed as a static JSON snapshot that the Next.js frontend consumes — no live API calls, instant load, free Vercel hosting.
